About us


Based on our experience, we know that immigration is not an easy process. That's why we've created SmartStream a platform designed to assist you throughout the journey. Our primary goal is to provide you with the right tools and guidance to navigate the pathway to achieving Canadian Permanent Residency (PR).
At SmartStream, we are committed to data privacy. We ensure that we only collect the minimal information required for login and never store more than it.
Furthermore, we understand that nowadays immigration programs are dynamic and constantly evolving. To keep you informed with the latest updates, we continuously refresh our information using sophisticated AI-driven techniques. This ensures that you always have access to the most current and relevant details.
